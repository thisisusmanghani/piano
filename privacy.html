Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to StringMaster ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our browser-based music production tools.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when you participate in activities on the website (such as using our Digital Studio), or otherwise when you contact us.

  • Personal Usage Data: Basic account information if you sign up.
  • Audio Data: Temporary local processing of your audio inputs for the tuner and tools. We do not store your audio recordings on our servers unless explicitly saved by you to your user profile.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for analytics and debugging.

3. Use of Your Information

We use personal information collected via our website for a variety of business purposes described below. We process your personal information for these purposes in reliance on our legitimate business interests, in order to enter into or perform a contract with you, with your consent, and/or for compliance with our legal obligations.

- To facilitate account creation and logon process.
- To send administrative information to you.
- To protect our Services.
- To improve our Audio Engine performance through anonymous usage statistics.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us at support@stringmaster.com
